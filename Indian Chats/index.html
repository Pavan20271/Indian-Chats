Pani Puri

Ingredients:

    For the Puris:

  • Semolina (sooji/rava)
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying

    • For the Pani

  • Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Lemon juice
  • Roasted cumin powder
  • Chaat masala
  • Tamarind pulp
  • Salt
  • Water

    • For the Filling:

  • Boiled potatoes
  • Onion
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Green chutney
  • Sev
  • Boondi
  • Coriander leaves

Instructions:

    Mix semolina, flour, and salt. Make a stiff dough, roll out small puris, and fry until golden. Blend mint, coriander, green chilies, ginger, lemon juice, cumin powder, chaat masala, tamarind pulp, salt, and water to make spicy water (pani). Mix boiled potatoes, chickpeas, onion for filling. Fill puris with the filling, add chutneys, dip in pani, garnish with sev, boondi, and coriander. Serve and enjoy fresh!

Bhel Puri

Ingredients:

  • Puffed rice (murmura)
  • Sev
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Boiled potato
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Green chutney
  • Chaat masala
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice
  • Coriander leaves

Instructions:

    In a large bowl, mix puffed rice, sev, chopped onion, tomato, and boiled potato. Add tamarind chutney and green chutney according to taste. Sprinkle chaat masala, salt, and lemon juice over the mixture. Mix everything well until evenly coated. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve immediately and enjoy the tangy and crunchy flavors of bhel puri!

Sev Puri

Ingredients:

  • Puri
  • Sev
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Boiled potato
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Green chutney
  • Chaat masala
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice
  • Coriander leaves

Instructions:

    Place puris on a plate. Top each puri with a spoonful of mashed boiled potatoes. Sprinkle chopped onion and tomato over the potatoes. Drizzle tamarind chutney and green chutney on top. Sprinkle chaat masala and salt according to taste. Generously garnish with sev. Finally, garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve immediately and enjoy the delightful flavors of sev puri!

Samosas

Ingredients:

    For the dough:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil or ghee
  • Salt
  • Water

    • For the filling:

  • Potato
  • Green peas
  • Onion
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Cumin seeds
  • Coriander powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

    Make a dough by mixing flour, salt, and oil; let it rest. Prepare filling: sauté onions, green chilies, add ginger-garlic paste, spices, peas, and mashed potatoes; cook until done. Roll dough into circles, cut in half, form cones, fill with the mixture, seal edges. Fry samosas in hot oil until golden brown. Drain excess oil and serve hot with chutney.

Baby Corn Manchurian

Ingredients:

  • Baby corn
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Soy sauce
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Capsicum
  • Onion
  • Tomato ketchup
  • Chili sauce

Instructions:

    Mix all batter ingredients (except water) to make a smooth batter, then coat baby corn pieces and fry until golden brown. In a pan, heat oil and sauté garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Add onion and capsicum, cook until tender. Stir in soy sauce, chili sauce, ketchup, vinegar, and cornstarch mixture. Cook until thickened. Add fried baby corn, coat in sauce, and cook briefly. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

Gobi Manchurian

Ingredients:

  • Cauliflower florets
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Soy sauce
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Capsicum
  • Onion
  • Tomato ketchup
  • Chili sauce

Instructions:

    Mix cauliflower with batter (flour, cornstarch, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, chili powder, salt). Fry cauliflower until golden brown, then set aside. Sauté garlic, ginger, green chilies, onion, and capsicum in oil. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, ketchup, vinegar, and cornstarch-water mixture. Cook until thickened. Add cauliflower to the sauce, coat evenly, and cook briefly. Garnish with spring onions. Serve hot as a side dish or appetizer.
